Is East Boston a Livable Neighborhood? And for How Long?

What Makes a Neighborhood Livable? Jane Jacobs argued that successful urban spaces share human scale, walkability, and a mixture of housing, shops, green space, and public buildings and also have sufficient building densities. "- The Death and Life of Great American Cities"

Does East Boston fit this idea of a livable community? And what effect will the changes that are coming to this gentifying neighborhood have on livability?
